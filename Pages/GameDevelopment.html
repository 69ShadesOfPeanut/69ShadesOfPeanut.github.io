These are my most recent game development projects that I have worked on using godot with it's native language, "GDScript"

Forest Wanderer

Made for the 2023 kenney jam, the game jam took place over roughly 2 days

Take on the role of someone who's entire job is to go searching into the dark woods at night in search of missing / lost items.
Learn not just to explore the forest but to respect it and its boundaries. And remember, the harder you push the harder that something is going to push back...

Dungeon Dash

Dungeon Dash was made as part of 26th score jam with the theme "Collect" within the time limit of 2 days

Explore a dungeon filled with treasure, collect coins to earn points which'll surely bring you great fame in the form of the online leader board!

Spectral Astro

Spectral Astro was a game made for the second game jam I participated in, the game jam being the enigma game jam
The game is 2D and based off the gameplay and art style of Asteroids.

Ohio Invasion: Conquest

Ohio Invasion was my second and latest project, being made for the godot wildjam
Making the game for a game jam helped me with time management and working in a team as I coded alongside a friend.
The gamejam ran for just over a week and everyone had to follow a theme which was "Assembly required"

Astrophobia

Astrophobia is my first game development project.
set in a world that's plunging into chaos, the player has their hands on a program made by SCI in order to recruit and train people to go on a space colonisation mission
Astrophobia took me just under a year to develop in my freetime while completing year 12 VCE
