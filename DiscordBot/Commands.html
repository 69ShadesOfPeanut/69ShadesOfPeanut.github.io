Fun Commands

Kill [user]
Kill a user of your choosing
Gay [user]
Tells you how gay a user of your choosing is
RandomCat
Sends a pic of a random Cat
RandomDog
Sends a pic of a random Dog

Utility Commands

About
Displays info about the bot
Avatar [user]
Gives you the avatar of a user of your choosing
UserInfo [user]
Gives you information about a specific user
ServerInfo
Gives you information about the server
Weather [location]
Gives you weather information about a specific area
Ping
Gives the ping / latency of the bot
